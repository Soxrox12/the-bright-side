Mental Health Resources

1. The American Foundation for suicide Prevention provides support groups, mental health professionals, resources on loss and suicide prevention information. Phone: 888-333-2377 Website: AFSP.org

2. The National Domestic Violence Hotline provides 24/7 crisis intervention, safety planning and information on domestic violence. Phone: 800-799-7233 Website: thehotline.org

3. The Sucide Prevention Lifeline connects callers to trained crisis counselors 24/7. They also provide a chat function on their website. Phone: 800-273-8255 Website: suicidepreventionlifeline.org

4. Depression and Bipolar Support Alliance(DBSA) provides information on bipolar disorder and depression, offers in-person and online support groups and forums. Phone: 800-826-3632 Website: dbsalliance.org

5. National Center of Excellence for Eating Disorders(NCEED) provides up-to-date, reliable and evidence-based information about eating disorders. Phone: 800-931-2237 Website: nceedus.org
